Why are we having this ball?

This years Christmas Ball is a fundraiser for our Christmas Fashion Show, donations and ticket money will help fund our art students debut of their recent designs for the annual Fashion Show. This is a formal event filled with music and dancing so feel encouraged to purchase tickets!
